DRC Education & Conflict Data Portal

This project monitors the impact of armed conflict on the educational infrastructure in the Democratic Republic of Congo. By cross-referencing over 7,900 school locations with nearly 10,000 georeferenced conflict events, we identify "at-risk" schools and provide actionable data for humanitarian prioritization and educational safety planning.
