Interactive Risk Map

Explore geolocated school data layered with real-time conflict event proximity. Identify vulnerable regions through high-precision heat mapping.

Analytical Dashboard

Analyze conflict severity trends, frequency of school-proximate events, and regional stability metrics through data-driven visualizations.

Data Sources

Our platform aggregates disaggregated event data from ACLED and UCDP, cross-referenced with educational facility points from GRID3 and OpenStreetMap. Risk analysis is calculated using a 10km proximity threshold via the Haversine formula.
